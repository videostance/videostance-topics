# Topic: Video Essay Creators Compared

Cross-analysis of long-form video essayists - narrative structure, research depth, and framing.

## Consensus

- The best essays lead with a thesis, not just a topic.
- Visual pacing matters more than production budget.

## Controversy

- Documentary neutrality vs. argumentative framing.

## Unique insights

- A few creators treat essays as peer-reviewed arguments with citations.
